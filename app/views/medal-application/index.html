Use this service to apply for medals awarded:

  • during your military career, but you don’t have
  • to someone you have lasting power of attorney for during their military career, but they don’t have
  • to a deceased family member during their career

This service only provides medals awarded by:

  • Army
  • Home guard
  • Royal air force
  • Royal Navy
  • Royal Marines
  • The reserve forces

If you’re apply for medals awarded to you, you’ll need details of your service career.

If you’re apply using lasting power of attorney you’ll need:

  • details of the person’s military career
  • evidence of lasting power of attorney

You can only apply for the medals of a deceased realtive if you are:

  • within two generations of the service person, for example you can apply for medals for a parent or grandparent but not great-grandparent
  • the closest living next of kin

Applying takes around 5 minutes.
